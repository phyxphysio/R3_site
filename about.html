About Us

Our Environmental Purpose

Our environmental purpose is embedded in our name and our operations: Reclamation, Recycling, Refinement, Reform, and Remediation. We are dedicated to not just minimizing environmental impact but actively improving the natural world through our work. It's about taking responsibility for the full lifecycle of the resources we use.

Our Approach
Our approach is encapsulated by a series of transformative actions: Restructure, Regenerate, Redeploy, Recoup, and Rectification. We remodel the very foundations of resource usage to replenish what has been depleted, refuel innovation, restock possibilities, restore ecosystems, and resurrect a harmonious balance between industry and environment. We are resolved to revitalise communities, rejuvenate habitats, refit industries with greener technologies, and reinvigorate economies with sustainable practices. At R3, we don't just adapt; we reinvent. We're not just recovering resources; we're redefining their potential.
